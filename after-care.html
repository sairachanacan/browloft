    AFTER CARE

    Proper aftercare helps protect your skin and maintain your results. Please follow the guidelines below for the best outcome after your treatment.

    Threading Aftercare

    Your skin may feel slightly sensitive immediately after threading. To protect and soothe the area:

    Post-Treatment Care
    • Avoid direct sun exposure for at least 2 hours after your appointment. If outdoors, apply sunscreen.
    • Stay away from heat sources such as saunas, tanning beds, hot baths, or steam for the rest of the day.
    • Avoid touching the treated area to prevent transferring oils or bacteria to freshly opened pores.
    • Use a gentle cleanser a few hours after treatment.
    • Avoid exfoliating products for 24 hours.

    Most clients schedule touch-up appointments every 2-4 weeks to maintain clean, defined brows.

    Brow & Lash Tint Aftercare

    To ensure long-lasting colour and comfort:

    Post-Treatment Care
    • Avoid touching or rubbing the treated area immediately after your appointment.
    • Refrain from using hot water, steam, or saunas on the face for 24 hours.
    • Avoid oil-based makeup removers, serums, or conditioners on brows or lashes.
    • If mild redness occurs, apply a cool compress to soothe the area.
    • If irritation continues beyond 24 hours, consult a healthcare professional.

    Tint results will gradually fade over several weeks depending on skin type and aftercare routine.

    Lash Lift Aftercare

    For best results during the first 24-48 hours:

    First 24 Hours
    • Keep lashes completely dry for the first 24 hours.
    • Avoid swimming, steam, or excessive moisture.
    • Do not rub or pull on lashes.
    • Avoid eye makeup for 24 hours.
    • Do not use waterproof mascara.

    After 24 hours, you may resume normal activities, though excessive steam can reduce longevity.

    Facial Aftercare

    To maintain your glow and protect your skin:

    Post-Treatment Care
    • Avoid applying makeup immediately after your facial.
    • Cleanse gently and keep skin hydrated with moisturizer.
    • Drink plenty of water to support skin recovery.
    • Avoid direct sun exposure and heat (sauna, tanning beds, hot showers) for 24-48 hours.
    • Do not use exfoliating products or active treatments for 72 hours.
    • Avoid additional facial treatments within 48 hours.
    • Do not apply perfume or fragranced products to treated areas for 48 hours.

    If you experience unusual irritation or have questions about your aftercare routine, please contact us. We're happy to guide you.
