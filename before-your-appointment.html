    BEFORE YOUR APPOINTMENT

    Everything you need to know to prepare for your hair removal service and get the best results.

    Benefits of Threading

    Eyebrow threading is a centuries-old technique cherished throughout Asia and the Middle East.

    Why Choose Threading?

    Known for its gentle and precise approach, threading is ideal for sensitive skin and serves as a safe alternative to waxing or tweezing. It removes hair without stripping the skin, minimizing irritation and protecting your complexion while delivering beautifully shaped brows.

    Key Benefits
    • Gentle on sensitive skin — no chemicals or hot wax applied
    • Precise shaping for clean, defined brows
    • No risk of burns or allergic reactions
    • Removes hair from the root for longer-lasting results
    • Safe for all skin types, including acne-prone skin
    • Quick and efficient — ideal for touch-ups between appointments

    Before Your Wax

    Before you get stripped, be sure to tick off all of the items on our checklist:

    Pre-Wax Checklist
    • Your hair is at least 1/4" long
    • It has been at least 4 weeks since your last wax or 3 weeks since you last shaved
    • You are not on any medication that causes the skin to be sensitive (Accutane, Retin-A, Hydroxy Acids)
    • You have not engaged in heavy exercise, swimming or suntanning 24 hours prior to service

    Helpful Tips

    Follow these tips for the smoothest experience and best results:

    Preparation Tips
    • Take an Ibuprofen 30-45 mins prior to service to reduce pain
    • Take a warm shower and lightly exfoliate the skin. This will provide an easier exit of the hair follicles and ensure a smooth wax.
    • Do not apply any creams, gels or oils that may prevent the wax from adhering to the hair
    • Keep the service area clean and dry
    • Do not drink alcohol before service. Stimulants in the system can cause the skin to be extra sensitive.
