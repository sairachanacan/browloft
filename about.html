    FAQ

    Does threading hurt?

    There may be mild discomfort during your first session, but most clients find it becomes easier with regular visits. Our technicians work quickly and precisely to minimize any sensitivity.

    How long do results last?

    Threading results typically last 3 to 4 weeks depending on your hair growth cycle. We recommend booking regular appointments to maintain your shape.

    Can I wear makeup after my appointment?

    We recommend waiting at least 2 to 3 hours before applying makeup to freshly threaded areas to avoid irritation. Mineral-based products are gentler if you need coverage sooner.

    Do I need to book in advance?

    Walk-ins are welcome based on availability, but we recommend booking online to secure your preferred time and avoid wait times.

    What is the difference between threading and waxing?

    Threading uses a twisted cotton thread to remove hair at the follicle level, offering more precision and less irritation than waxing. It's ideal for sensitive skin and gives cleaner, sharper brow shapes.

    After Care
    • Avoid touching or rubbing the treated area for at least 2 hours after your service.
    • Stay away from direct sunlight, saunas, and steam rooms for 24 hours.
    • Skip exfoliating products, retinols, and AHAs around the area for 48 hours.
    • Apply aloe vera gel or a gentle soothing cream if you experience any redness — it usually subsides within a few hours.
    • Avoid swimming pools or hot tubs for 24 hours to prevent irritation from chlorine.
    • If you had a tint or lash lift, avoid getting the area wet for the first 24 hours for best results.
