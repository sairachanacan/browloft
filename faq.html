    FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

    What is eyebrow threading?

    Eyebrow threading is a precise hair-removal technique that originated in Asia and the Middle East centuries ago. It is a gentle alternative to waxing and tweezing, making it especially suitable for sensitive skin.

    Unlike waxing, threading does not remove a layer of skin. Instead, it targets individual hairs to create clean, defined brows that beautifully frame the face.

    How does threading work?

    During threading, a trained brow specialist uses a fine cotton thread that is twisted and rolled across the skin. As the thread moves, it gently traps and removes unwanted hair directly from the root with exceptional precision.

    This technique allows for detailed shaping and symmetrical brow definition.

    Is threading painful?

    Most clients find threading to be more comfortable than waxing. Because it removes individual hairs without pulling on the surrounding skin, irritation is typically minimal.

    If you have sensitive skin or are reactive to wax products, threading can be an excellent alternative.

    How long does threading take?

    • Eyebrow threading: Approximately 10–15 minutes
    • Full face threading: Approximately 30–45 minutes

    Service time may vary slightly depending on hair density and shaping needs.

    What areas can be threaded?

    Threading can be performed on:

    • Eyebrows
    • Upper lip
    • Chin
    • Sideburns
    • Neck
    • Forehead
    • Cheeks

    Is threading better than waxing?

    Threading offers several advantages over waxing and tweezing:

    • No hot wax or chemicals applied to the skin
    • More precise shaping
    • Minimal skin irritation
    • Hair is removed from the root for longer-lasting results

    For many clients, threading provides a cleaner finish with less discomfort.

    What are the benefits of threading?

    • Precise brow shaping from both above and below the brow line
    • Gentle on delicate facial skin
    • Suitable for sensitive skin types
    • Less redness compared to waxing
    • Targets even the finest hairs
    • Over time, hair may grow back finer and softer
    • 100% natural technique — no artificial products used

    How long does Brow Tint last?

    Brow tint typically lasts 4–6 weeks, gradually fading over time depending on your skin type, hair growth cycle, and aftercare.

    How long does Lash Tint last?

    Lash tint results generally last 6–8 weeks, depending on your natural lash cycle and maintenance routine.

    What are the benefits of Lash Lift & Tint?

    • Enhances natural lashes without extensions
    • Smudge-proof and waterproof
    • No need for mascara or lash curlers
    • Great for travel, sports, and everyday convenience
    • Low-maintenance beauty solution
    • Suitable for contact lens wearers
    • Formulated without harsh ingredients
